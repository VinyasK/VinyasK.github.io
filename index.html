Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole range of Indo-Islamic architecture. Its rhythmic combination of solids and voids, concave and convex and light shadow elevates its aesthetic. Not just a building — it’s a monument of eternal love.
